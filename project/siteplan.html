Website Planning Document

Site Name

Name: The Foodstead

Reason for Name: I wanted something that feels like homesteading. Like, intentional living and sustainable lifestyle. I also wanted it to hint at the food aspect

Site Purpose

The Foodstead provides a hub for consumers to discover sustainable local food sources, including farmers' markets, CSAs, co-ops, and restaurants that source locally. Users can search by location, filter by type, view interactive maps, and save their favorites.

Scenarios

  • Where can I find a farmers' market near me that is open this weekend?
  • Which local restaurants source food from nearby farms?
  • How do I join a CSA program in my area?

Color Schema

Primary Color - #4E6E5D Used for headings, navigation, and footer background.

Secondary Color - #B95E62 Used for call-to-action buttons and section borders.

Accent Color - #E3A857 Used for highlights, links, and hover effects.

Background Color - #F1EFE8 Used for page background.

Highlight Color - #ACECA1 Used for feature sections and emphasis areas.

Typography

Google Sans: Used for headings (h1, h2, h3) for a modern, clean brand look.

Sora: Used for body text, navigation links, buttons, and form text for readability.
